How to Use

For analysing a text, go to text analysis tab and enter your text in the box. After entering your text, just press the search button.

After pressing search, you will be redirected to the result page. There will be a graph of feeling from the text you have entered.

To analyse twitter hashtags or keywords, go to twitter analysis tab. There you will find two search bars, keyword and number of tweets. Enter your hashtag in keyword bar and enter number of tweets that you want to be fetched in next bar. Then, just press search button.

After pressing search, you will be redirected to the result page. There you will see two graphs. One is for feelings analysis which will show the extent of feelings from fetched tweets and the other one is a pie diagram which will show the extent of positivity and negativity from the tweets. From these two plots, you will be familiar with how people are reacting to a particular hashtag.

If you have any queries or suggestions, feel free to drop it in contact section.
